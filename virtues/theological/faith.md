# Faith

## Type
Theological Virtue

## Definition
Faith is the theological virtue by which we believe in God
and believe all that He has said and revealed to us.

## Key Relationships
- Perfected by: Charity
- Supported by: Hope
- Weakened by: Doubt, Presumption
- Requires: Humility, Docility
