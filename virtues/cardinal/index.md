# Cardinal Virtues

The cardinal virtues are the principal moral virtues on which many other virtues depend.

## Included Virtues

- [Prudence](prudence.md)
- [Justice](justice.md)
- [Temperance](temperance.md)
- [Fortitude](fortitude.md)

## Short Descriptions

- **Prudence** — right reason in action.
- **Justice** — giving to each what is due.
- **Temperance** — moderating attraction to pleasures.
- **Fortitude** — firmness in difficulties and constancy in the pursuit of the good.
